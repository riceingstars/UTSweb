Profile

Made by Siti Fauziah

Artlantiest is a creative venture dedicated to bringing unique, handmade beadwork and trinkets to life. Founded in 2024, Artlantiest started as a passion project and side job, offering a wide range of customized bead accessories, jewelry, and crafts. With a focus on artistry, quality, and affordability, Artlantiest provides a space for artistic expression and craftsmanship, crafting each piece with care and attention to detail. The store welcomes requests for bespoke designs, aiming to fulfill individual tastes and preferences, ensuring that every customer receives a product that speaks to their personal style.
