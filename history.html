History

This is Our History...

Artlantiest was founded in 2024 out of a deep love for crafting and an appreciation for the timeless beauty of beads. What began as a hobby turned into a thriving side business, where creativity and customer satisfaction became the core values. With a commitment to making beadwork accessible to all, Artlantiest quickly built a reputation for its high-quality handmade jewelry and accessories. The shop's collection ranges from intricate necklaces to custom earrings, bracelets, and home decor, each crafted with precision and passion.
